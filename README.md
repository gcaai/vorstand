## Dr. Han Xiao
#### English
Dr. Han Xiao is a senior staff scientist at Tencent AI Lab. He worked as a senior research scientist at Zalando research,  recommendation and search teams for 3.5 years. Han completed his Ph.D. and M.Sc. in computer science at Technical University of Munich, and B.Eng at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications. His focus is deep learning system in production.

#### Chinese
